Hospital Guidelines

  • Review all appointment requests regularly.
  • Approve appointments if the slot is available.
  • Reject appointments if the patient selects an invalid time.
  • Mark appointment as Completed after consultation.
  • Do not modify patient records without authorization.
  • Maintain professional conduct during consultations.

Appointment Status Guide

Pending

Patient has requested an appointment. Review and approve or reject.

Approved

The appointment is confirmed. The patient will visit at scheduled time.

Rejected

The appointment request is denied due to scheduling or other reasons.

Completed

Mark appointment completed after consultation with the patient.

Doctor Responsibilities

Doctors are responsible for reviewing appointment requests, managing their consultation schedule, and ensuring that patients receive appropriate medical care.

Please ensure that all appointments are updated with the correct status to maintain accurate hospital records.
